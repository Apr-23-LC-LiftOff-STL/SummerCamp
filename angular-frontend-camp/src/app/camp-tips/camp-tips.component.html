Camp Tips

  • Search and apply early-

    • Most camps have limited capacity and could be full if you're applying last minute.

  • Look for camps in areas of interest-

    • Participating in camps during free time is a great way to either learn new something totally new or to further improve in subjects where there is already interest.

  • Consider time-

    • Make sure to take in to consideration parent work schedules if a ride is needed.
