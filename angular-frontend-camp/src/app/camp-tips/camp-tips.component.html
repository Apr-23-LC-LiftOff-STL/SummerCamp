Camp Tips

  1. Search and apply early-

    2. Most camps have limited capacity and could be full if you're applying last minute.

  2. Look for camps in areas of interest-

    3. Participating in camps during free time is a great way to either learn new something totally new or to further improve in subjects where there is already interest.

  3. Consider start and end times-

    4. Make sure to take in to consideration parent work schedules if a ride is needed.

  4. Take note of dates and locations of camps-

    5. Some camps are only held during the daytime while others are overnight camps for a longer period of time. Some camps are have a further drive than others and it may be helpful to find a carpool to get there. Make sure you know what you're signing up for!

  5. Ask a friend to join you-

    6. It can be fun to go to camp on your own and make new friends, but it can be equally as fun to share the camp info with a friend if they have similar interests!

  6. Prepare camp directors with any concerns-

    7. If your child has any allergies or concerns about camp, it's helpful to contact the directors to get advice adhead of time.
